# Unscramble Word App Branding Guide

## Project Vision

Build a platform that combines:

* Word discovery
* Vocabulary learning
* Word games
* Educational content
* Brain training

The platform should appeal to:

* Kids
* Students
* Teachers
* Parents
* Word game enthusiasts
* Casual users

---

# Brand Personality

The brand should feel:

* Smart
* Friendly
* Helpful
* Modern
* Educational
* Fun

The brand should avoid:

* Looking childish
* Looking corporate
* Looking overly academic

---

# Logo Concepts

## Concept 1 – Letter Transform (Recommended)

Visual Concept:

A E L P P → APPLE

Represents:

* Chaos to Order
* Puzzle to Solution
* Letters to Words

Advantages:

* Instantly explains the product
* Works for kids and adults
* Supports future games and educational features
* Strong favicon potential

Recommended Priority:

#1

# Recommended Direction

* Word Games
* Daily Challenges
* Educational Content
* Brain Training

# Branding Success Criteria

The logo should:

* Be recognizable at favicon size
* Work on desktop and mobile
* Be understandable without explanation
* Support educational and gaming audiences
* Remain relevant as the platform expands
