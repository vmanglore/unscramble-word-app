Purpose

This roadmap defines the long-term evolution of Unscramble Word Now.

The objective is not simply to build features.

The objective is to evolve from a word-solving utility into a word discovery and knowledge platform.

This roadmap should guide prioritization, architecture decisions, and future investments.

# Roadmap Prioritization Rules

Features should be prioritized according to the following order.

---

## Priority 1

Strengthen the core experience.

Examples:

* Faster search
* Better rankings
* Better definitions

---

## Priority 2

Expand discovery.

Examples:

* Internal linking
* Related words
* Word pages

---

## Priority 3

Strengthen the Word Knowledge Layer.

Examples:

* Metadata
* Categories
* Relationships

---

## Priority 4

Expand educational value.

Examples:

* Vocabulary tools
* Learning content

---

## Priority 5

Experimental features.

Only after core objectives are met.

---

# Features Not Currently Prioritized

The following may be valuable in the future but are not current priorities.

Examples:

* Accounts
* Social features
* User-generated content
* AI-powered features without clear value
* Complex gamification

These should not distract from the core roadmap.

# Success Definition

The project succeeds when it evolves beyond:

```text
"What words can I make?"
```

and helps users answer:

```text
"What does this word mean?"

"How useful is this word?"

"What words are related?"

"What should I learn next?"
```

The long-term goal is not merely to generate words.

The long-term goal is to build the most useful word discovery platform on the web.

---

# Guiding Principle

Every release should improve the current product while moving the platform closer to its long-term vision.
