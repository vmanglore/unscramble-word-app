# ROADMAP_BACKLOG_ENHANCEMENTS_V01

Status: Research

Version: 1.0

Purpose:

This document captures roadmap and backlog enhancement opportunities identified during competitor analysis, navigation planning, Word Discovery Platform discussions, Word Knowledge Layer planning, and future WordNavic vision exploration.

This document does not modify the roadmap or backlog directly.

Its purpose is to evaluate potential additions before promotion into official planning documents.

---

# Background

The current roadmap correctly defines the long-term evolution of the platform:

Word Solver
↓
Word Discovery Platform
↓
Word Knowledge Platform
↓
Language Discovery Platform

Recent analysis suggests opportunities to strengthen future discovery, educational, gaming, and navigation capabilities while remaining aligned with the existing roadmap.

---

# Key Observation

Most current competitors focus on solving words.

Long-term differentiation will likely come from helping users:

* Discover words
* Understand words
* Learn words
* Explore relationships between words

rather than simply generating words.
