# Common Industry Weaknesses

## Flat Result Lists

Observation:

Many competitors display large result sets as a single flat list.

Example:

132 words found

word1
word2
word3
...

Impact:

Difficult to scan.

Opportunity:

* Group by length
* Collapsible sections
* Recommended results

Priority:

High

---

## No Recommended Results

Observation:

Most competitors display all words equally.

Impact:

Users must determine which words are most useful.

Opportunity:

Recommended ranking engine.

Priority:

High

---

## Limited Sorting

Observation:

Most competitors offer few or no sorting options.

Impact:

Different users have different objectives.

Opportunity:

Support:

* Recommended
* Most Common
* Longest
* Highest Score
* A-Z
* Rare Words

Priority:

High

---

## Weak Mobile UX

Observation:

Large result sets become difficult to navigate on mobile devices.

Opportunity:

* Mobile-first layouts
* Collapsible sections
* Sticky result controls

Priority:

High

---

## Minimal Word Intelligence

Observation:

Most competitors display words only.

Opportunity:

Display:

* Definition
* Scrabble score
* Length
* Frequency
* Related words

Priority:

Medium

## Opportunity 5

Advanced Word Discovery

Description:

Move beyond a traditional unscrambler.

Examples:

* Exactly N vowels
* No vowels
* Exactly N consonants
* Repeated letters only
* Score ranges
* Frequency ranges

Potential Impact:

High

1.4+
