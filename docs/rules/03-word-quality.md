# Word Quality Rules

WQ-001
Meaningful English words are preferred.

Examples:

Preferred:
stream
master
steam
teams
stare

Not Preferred:
mr
sr
rt
rs
mt

WQ-002
Abbreviations should be excluded unless explicitly approved.

WQ-003
Dictionary fragments should be excluded.

WQ-004
Ranking should prioritize:

1. Common English words
2. Frequently used words
3. Longer words
4. Rare words

WQ-005
Results should be useful to humans,
not only valid Scrabble entries.
